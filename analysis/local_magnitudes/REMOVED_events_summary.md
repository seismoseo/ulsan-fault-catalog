## Criterion (one line)
Reject an event if **fewer than half its PhaseNet+ detection-pick stations fit a single P-moveout
within 1 s of its HYPOINVERSE location** — `mv_inlier < 0.5`, on events with ≥5 P picks.
Robust to the fraction of bad picks any AI picker emits (a few outliers leave the majority fitting;
only a wrong *location* knocks the bulk off). Uses **PhaseNet+ data only** — no other picker.

## Why standard QC misses these
HYPOINVERSE `rms/erh/erz/qual` are a within-model fit on the *picks it kept*, with freedom in depth +
origin-time; a wide-gap event dives deep and absorbs timing scatter into a low RMS for a wrong location.
The moveout of **all** detection picks against the reported hypocentre is independent of that fit.
